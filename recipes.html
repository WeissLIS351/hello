Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread
  • jar of peanut butter (any kind is fine, use what you like)
  • jar of jam or jelly of your choice (grape jelly is a classic)

Method:

Using a knife spread about 2 tbs of peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread. Spread 2 tbs of jelly or jam on one side of the other slice of bread. Put the slices of bread together with the jam and peanut butter sides touching. Enjoy!

Variations:

  • Instead of using jam or jelly, put bananna slices on top of the peanut butter. You can also add a little cinnamon or sugar if you like.
  • Use honey instead of jam or jelly.
  • Slice your finished sandwich into quarters for mini PB&J sliders.
